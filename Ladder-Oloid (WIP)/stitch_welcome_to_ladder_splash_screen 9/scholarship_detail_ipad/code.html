About the Program

The Meridian Global Scholars Grant was established to empower the next generation of visionary leaders who demonstrate a profound commitment to sustainable development and cross-cultural communication. This prestigious award goes beyond financial assistance, providing recipients with a dedicated mentor from our global alumni network and exclusive access to the annual Meridian Leadership Summit in Geneva.
