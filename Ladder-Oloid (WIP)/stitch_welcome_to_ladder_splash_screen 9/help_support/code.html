Frequently Asked Questions

How is match score calculated? expand_more
Our proprietary algorithm considers 147 unique academic and lifestyle markers, including study habits, deadline proximity, and collaborative temperament to ensure high-fidelity roommate and mentor pairing.
Is my data safe? expand_more
We utilize 256-bit AES encryption for all personal and academic records. Your data is stored in localized nodes and is never sold to third-party academic recruiters.
How do I connect with my counselor? expand_more
Navigate to the "Consultation" tab in your profile. You can schedule 15-minute syncs or longer 1-hour strategic planning sessions based on counselor availability.
Can my parent see my essays? expand_more
Privacy is paramount. Parents only have access to financial deadlines and housing statuses unless you explicitly grant "Draft View" permissions in your Privacy Settings.
